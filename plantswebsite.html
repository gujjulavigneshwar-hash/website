Plant Care Tips

Keep your plants happy and healthy

💧

Watering

Most indoor plants need watering once or twice a week. Always check the soil before watering — if the top inch is dry, it's time to water.

🌤️

Sunlight

Place plants near bright windows but avoid harsh direct afternoon sun. Most houseplants prefer indirect or filtered light throughout the day.

🌡️

Temperature

Most plants thrive between 18°C – 30°C. Avoid placing them near air conditioners, heaters, or drafty windows during seasonal changes.
